Kibibi Model Secondary School is a day and boarding private school located in Gombe Town Council, Butambala District, within walking distance of Gombe Hospital. We believe in the holistic development of a child and ensure a balance between academics, sport, culture, MDD, and discipline. We are a secondary school that provides a firm background for the development of skills and talents for academic progress. We boast of fully functional laboratories as well as spacious classrooms in a quiet environment. The school follows the New Competency Based Curriculum. We admit students from S.1 - S.5 .

Headteacher's Message

Dear Students, Parents, and Visitors,

It is my pleasure to welcome you to Kibibi Model Secondary School, a vibrant learning community located in the heart of Gombe Town Council. As the Headteacher, I am proud to lead an institution that values excellence, innovation, and holistic education.

At Kibibi Model Secondary School, we are committed to nurturing the talents and aspirations of every student. Our rigorous academic programs are complemented by a wide array of extracurricular activities designed to inspire creativity, leadership, and social responsibility. With a team of dedicated teachers and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to provide an environment where every learner can thrive and reach their full potential.

We believe that education is a partnership between the school, parents, and the community. Together, we aim to instill in our students the values of discipline, respect, and hard work. Our goal is to prepare them not only for academic success but also for the challenges and opportunities of a rapidly changing world.

Thank you for your continued support. I invite you to explore our website and learn more about the exciting programs and opportunities available at Kibibi Model Secondary School.

Warm regards.
