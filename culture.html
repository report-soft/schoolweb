Life at School

Student life in KMSS varies from one student to another. The activities vary and are engaging as most of the remarkable students we have not only work hard in class but also take part in other activities like sports and athletes, music and drama and many other student based activities. We have a population of slightly over 300 students attending their Uganda Certificate and Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education levels.

School Uniform

Our school uniform comprises of the actual uniform (sky blue dress, sky blue shirt, grey sweater, & grey trousers), casual wear (navy blue skirts and trousers, blue t-shirts), and Sportswear. You supposed to wear only clothes issued by the school.

Spirituality and Religion

We respect all religions. On Sundays, students worship God in their respective religions. Muslims go for Juma prayers at the Mosque on Friday. Religion inculcates values such as respect, godliness, hard work, patience, and integrity.
