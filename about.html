School Background

Kibibi Model Secondary School is a day and boarding private school located in Gombe Town Council, Butambala District, within walking distance of Gombe Hospital. We believe in the holistic development of a child and ensure a balance between academics, sport, culture, MDD, and discipline. We are a secondary school that provides a firm background for the development of skills and talents for academic progress. We boast of fully functional laboratories as well as spacious classrooms in a quiet environment. The school follows the New Competency Based Curriculum. We admit students from S.1 - S.5 .

School Administration

The School Administrative team is headed by Dr. Mutetikka David (The Headteacher), assisted by two deputy headteachers. The deputy in-charge of administration and discipline and The deputy Head teacher in-charge of Academics, Joseph Kato. The administrative team is also composed of the Director of Studies, Mr. Ssempagala Robert.

The above team forms the top administration of the school. Besides the top administration, there are various Heads of Departments for different subjects, club patrons, the Senior Woman, the School Sheikh and the Priest, as well as the non-teaching staff who aid in administration.

School Mission:

"To develop academically competent individuals with capacity to solve life challenges. We aim at providing a curriculum that will enable our students to not only pass the national examinations at the end of the four/two years, but also to be able to solve challenges in real life outside school."

School Vision:

"A learning institution that provides a firm background for the development of skills and talents for academic progress."
